The Best Movie According to Irfan (D22215002)

My top 3 of all-time.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol 3

I love the humor, action, and unique characters that make this superhero film stand out from others. It's a fun and entertaining ride from start to finish.

Avatar

Breathtaking visual effects and immersive world, taking me on an unforgettable journey to a stunning alien planet filled with fascinating creatures and cultures.

Up

Heartwarming and touching story that reminds me of the importance of friendship, adventure, and never giving up on our dreams.
